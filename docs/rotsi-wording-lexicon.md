# Rotsi Wording Lexicon

This file keeps the public site language consistent.

## Preferred Terms

Use these when they fit naturally:
- Rotsi
- technical partner
- business automation systems
- custom platforms
- workflow infrastructure
- internal platform
- role-based permissions
- operational reporting
- inventory visibility
- customer records
- abandoned cart recovery
- loyalty mechanics
- SEO content engine
- WhatsApp workflow automation

## Preferred Tone

- direct
- practical
- confident
- specific
- commercially aware

The site should sound like a team that builds serious systems, not a design studio trying to sound profound.

## Avoid Or Replace

Avoid these terms unless there is a very clear reason:
- architectural excellence
- modern lever
- flow-state systems
- cognitive lever
- elite growth
- high-performance operational design
- redefining the digital foundation
- precision-engineered systems for elite growth

These phrases make the brand sound abstract and over-written. They also weaken SEO because they do not match how people search for the work.

## Writing Rules

- Lead with the business problem or system type
- Explain what was built in plain language
- Mention mechanisms when they matter
- Keep proof points factual and restrained
- Prefer one clear sentence over stacked rhetoric
